VANILLA ICE CREAM

Vanilla is the king of ice cream flavors. This is the best homemade vanilla ice cream I've ever had. The honey and brown sugar give it a rich, buttery taste that can't be beat.

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient Instruction
2 cups milk
6 egg yolks
5 spoons honey
1/4 spoon salt
2 cups cream heavy cream
7 inch vanilla bean minced

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a medium pan, heat the milk and vanilla bean to simmering. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, brown sugar, honey, and salt until light colored and frothy.While whisking constantly, slowly combine the hot milk with the egg mixture. Transfer the mixture back to the sauce pan.
  2. Cook over medium heat until mixture reaches 82 degree, or becomes thick enough to coat the back of a metal spoon. Remove from heat.
  3. Remove the vanilla bean from the mixture and reserve. Strain custard into a large bowl. Scrape seeds from the vanilla bean into the custard. (Vanilla bean pod may be used again.) Stir the heavy cream into the custard. Cover the surface of the custard with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  4. When cold, freeze in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer's directions.
