HOT DOG FRIED RICE

Fried rice with hot dogs starts with prepared rice combined with peas, carrots, frank pieces and scrambled Egg Beaters for a quick weeknight recipe.

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient Instruction
2 spoons canola oil
3 cups cooked rice white long
2 pieces hotdogs sliced
2 spoons soy sauce
3/4 cup egg beaters fried
1 cup peas & carrots unfrozen

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat oil in large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add rice,cook 5 to 7 minutes or until hot and lightly browned, stirring occasionally. Remove from skillet; set aside
  3. Add frank pieces; cook 2 to 3 minutes or until browned, stirring occasionally. Add peas and carrots; cook 2 minutes more or until hot. Add rice and soy sauce; stir to combine
  4. Push rice mixture to one side of skillet. Add Egg Beaters to empty side; cook without stirring until edges and bottom begin to set.
  5. Gently turn to scramble; continue cooking until set. Stir scrambled Egg Beaters into rice mixture.
